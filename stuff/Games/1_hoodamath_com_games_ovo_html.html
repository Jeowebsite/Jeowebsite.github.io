OvO Instructions
In this platformer game, you control your character using the arrow keys. You can move left and right with the left and right arrow keys and jump by pressing the up arrow key. Jumping is essential for clearing obstacles and reaching higher platforms. To add some depth to your movement, you can also slide by pressing the down arrow key while running. Sliding helps you pass through tight spaces and avoid obstacles. For extra distance in the air, you can jump while sliding, which is particularly useful for covering larger gaps between platforms. Additionally, you have the ability to perform a smash attack by pressing the down arrow key while in the air. This smash attack allows you to pass through transparent platforms or destroy obstacles in your path. After smashing, you can jump immediately to gain extra height, which is beneficial for reaching high platforms. If you encounter vertical walls, you can execute a wall jump by pressing the up arrow key while sliding down the wall. Wall jumps come in handy when you need to scale vertical surfaces or reach new areas. Your main objective is to reach the flag as quickly as possible while navigating through various challenges. Collecting bonus coins will unlock different character skins, which you can select from the main menu to customize your in-game appearance. Mastering these controls and actions is essential to efficiently progress through the game, overcome obstacles, and complete levels. So, enjoy the game, and aim to achieve the fastest time to the finish while collecting those bonus coins for cool character skins!
